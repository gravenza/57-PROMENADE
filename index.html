WHY
5IFTY 7EVEN PROMENADE ?

Fifty Seven Promenade, strategically located in the very heart of CBD, is a luxury waterfront living, with al fresco dining, lifestyle retail and the best in amenities and facilities for both living, working and leisure. Easily accessible to North, South, West and East Jakarta, with excellent public transport links via Trans Jakarta Bus-way, Rail network and future MRT, LRT and Airport Express network.
